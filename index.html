About The Theater

World Theatre Day, celebrated annually on March 27th, is a significant event that honors the art of theatre and its impact on culture and society. The day was initiated in 1962 by the International Theatre Institute (ITI) and is marked by theatre communities around the globe1. Theatre, as an art form, has the power to entertain, educate, and inspire. It serves as a mirror to society, reflecting its virtues and vices, and has the unique ability to bring people together, transcending boundaries and creating a shared experience. World Theatre Day celebrates this spirit of unity and the transformative power of theatre.
